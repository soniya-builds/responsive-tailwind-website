Frequently asked questions

Is ToDesktop For Me?

If you want to distribute your web app as a downloadable desktop app for your users, ToDesktop is perfect for you.

How long does setup take?

You can have your first desktop app running in under 10 minutes. Just paste your web app URL and you're done.

Which platforms are supported?

ToDesktop supports Mac, Windows, and Linux. Build for all three platforms from a single codebase with no extra effort.

Do I need to know Electron?

No! ToDesktop handles all the Electron complexity. Zero Electron knowledge needed, and add native features progressively as you grow.

Can I migrate my existing Electron app?

Yes! ToDesktop is fully compatible with Electron. Migrate your existing app and start using ToDesktop's features immediately.

What support is available?

We offer email support for all plans. Professional and Enterprise plans include priority support and Slack channels.
